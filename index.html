01 THE "WHY"
What is the value of a thought if it isn’t shared? I believe the true power of game development lies in the ability to transfer a specific vibe or a fleeting emotion directly to another person. I started this journey to build experiences that act as a bridge between my imagination and the player’s reality. Seeing someone find genuine joy or connection in what I’ve created is what turns my passion into a lifelong commitment.
02 QUEST LOG

NOTICED

3rd Place! My solo survival experiment. A journey into the seen and unseen.

I faced the ghost of FOMO and won. After two days of mental paralysis, I pushed myself to see if I could build a bug-free experience solo. This project became a canvas for testing my resilience and speed, proving that my inner dev is a force to be reckoned with. It's more than just a game; it's a testament to solo grit.

Stable Build 02

48 hours of chaos. 1st Place Most Liked by the community!

My first time working with a professional team in a high-pressure environment. I went from being nervous to being absolutely blown away by the power of UML diagrams and modular Scriptable Objects. I handled both the coding architecture and the voice-over performance—teaching me that great risks lead to the most rewarding growth.

Kitchen Chaos

From "just working" to "working professionally." The architectural leap.

This project was my ultimate technical level-up. I stopped focusing on just the 'output' and started caring deeply about the infrastructure. Implementing State Machines to handle burning food logic made me realize that even simple interactions require a beautiful, clean architecture. I finally felt like a software architect.

Oneiros

My very first game! Born from sleepless nights and curious dreams.

Inspired by the surreal science of dreams, Oneiros was the spark that started it all. This project transformed me from a passive learner into an active creator who wanted to share knowledge with the world. Ranked 2nd by my peers, it taught me that a compelling narrative can create something truly magical from scratch.

Multiplayer XO

Connecting worlds. Making screens talk to each other in real-time.

This was my "Hello World" to the complex realm of networking. Navigating client-server synchronization felt like learning a new language. There is no feeling quite like the adrenaline rush of seeing that first synchronized "X" appear instantly on two different screens! It opened my eyes to social potential.

COMING SOON
